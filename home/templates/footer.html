Windshild Autoglass Limited is an officially registered company in Kenya, specifically in Nairobi city's Industrial Area on Bunyala Road. Established in July 2018, Windshild Autoglass Limited operates as a limited liability company with a primary focus on providing specialized services in the replacement of motor vehicle windshields, sidefixes, quarter glass, door frames, and various spare parts.
